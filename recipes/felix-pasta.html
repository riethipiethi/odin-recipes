Felix's Pasta

Description

This is one of my go-to recipes. It is very simple but extremely tasty.

Ingredients

  • 250g pasta (of any sort)
  • 100g sun-dried tomatoes in oil
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 onion
  • 100g corn
  • 50g walnuts
  • Salt and pepper
  • Pesto

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salt water as described on the package.
  2. Cut the zucchini in small pieces and fry it in some of the oil of the dried tomatoes. Add onion and tomatoes cut into small pieces. After some minutes add the corn.
  3. Roast the walnuts in a separate pan and season them with salt and pepper
  4. Serve the pasta on a deep plate with pesto and top everything with the fried vegetables and roasted walnuts.
