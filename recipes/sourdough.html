Sourdough

Description

Baking sourdough bread for me is not only about the food. It's also about the process of baking and caring for your sourdough. In this recipe I will share with you my way of making my first sourdough and how I take care of it :)

Starting your first sourdough

Ingredients

  • 1 glass jar (minimum 400ml)
  • 1 kitchen cloth
  • 100g rye flour (full grain if possible)
  • 100ml water

Steps

  1. Mix water and rye flour in the glass jar. For stirring I prefer to use wooden spoons, but everything works.
  2. Cover the jar with the kitchen cloth and let it rest in a warm spot for some days. You can check in between if things are starting to become bubbly.
  3. The sourdough is ready to use when it has grown to about twice its original size.
  4. Let's bake some bread!
  5. When you're done with the starter close the lid of the jar and put it in your fridge. Now you only need to feed it every 7-10 days.

Feeding your sourdough

Ingredients

  • 2 Ts rye flour (full grain if possible)
  • 2 Ts water

Steps

  1. Mix water and rye flour with your sourdough.
  2. Cover the jar with the kitchen cloth and let it rest in a warm spot for 6-8 hours.
  3. The sourdough is ready to use when it has grown to about twice its original size.
  4. Let's bake some bread!
