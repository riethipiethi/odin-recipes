Sourdough bread

Description

Sourdough bread is one of the most tasty breads I have tried. Plus it is super fun to make yourself. Today I want to share with you my go-to sourdough bread recipe.

Ingredients

  • 500 g sourdough
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 Tbsp Salt
  • 200 g rye flour
  • 300 g wheat flour
  • 200 ml water

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a big bowl until you have a rough loaf. Afterwards knit the loaf for a few minutes on the kitchen counter until it becomes nice and smooth. If needed spray some extra flour on the kitchen counter so the dough does not stick to it.
  2. Form a nice loaf and put it on an oven tray with baking paper. Now cover the loaf with a kitchen towel or some other cloth and let it rest until the loaf has grown to about twice the original size. This can take up to 6 hours depending on temperature and flour used.
  3. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C. Put a small bowl with water next to the bread and bake it for about 60 minutes. Knocking on the bread should give a hollow sound when the bread is done. Let it now cool out on an oven grate.
